I used to work in an intensive care unit and loved it — helping others in a fast pased environment was my thing. But I've always had an urge to create things and done so in my spare time.

Being the perfect nurse while maintaining and improving my creative skillset was a bit too strenous which is why I'm now coding and designing fulltime at Socialsquare in Copenhagen, Denmark.

It's been a fun transition — turns out treating and caring for the critically ill is one of the most agile processes around if you just paraphrase it slightly:

Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
Living patients over comprehensive documentation
Patient collaboration over treatment negotiation
Responding to change over following a plan
